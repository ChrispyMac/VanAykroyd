A Couple of Vannies

Bio

Pamela and Chrispy are A Couple of Vannies, travelling across the UK and Europe in their converted van known as Van Aykroyd.

Van Aykroyd in all his shiny glory!

Van's Features

  • Originally a Ford Transit T280 stripped out and insulated.
  • Two-hob gas cooker hooked up to gas tank.
  • Sink drawing from water tank, draining out of waste pipe underneath the van.
  • Mini fridge connected to leisure battery.
  • Two USB charge sockets connected to leisure battery.
  • Two foam seats which convert into a double bed.
  • Space to store collapsible table.
  • Composting toilet with re-painted woodwork and side caddies for storage, waste bin, toilet roll etc.
